2016.5.15
President Bing Wu was re-elected for the second term of the Presidency.
Prof Sanbing Shen has decided to step down as a board member. His valuable contribution to the Association was deeply appreciated and formally acknowledged at the AGM.
The other six nominations were approved at the meeting. They were Jiahuai Wang, Helen Chen, Yuhui Gao, Wenxin Wang, Guangbo Hao, Xinmin Zhan. The Chair welcomed the new Board members Wenxin, Guangbo and Xinmin to join the Board.
